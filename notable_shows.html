Notable Shows

March 17, 2023 - Glendale, AZ

The first Eras Tour show! Every song was a surprise song for that crowd. There had been very little information about the tour's production and much speculation about which songs were going to be played. The crowd was left in amazement after over three amazing hours of performance.

May 5, 2023 - Nashville, TN

Before singing the first surprise song during the acoustic set, Taylor announced that she had been planning something special for a while and she was finally able to tell everyone about it. She asked the crowd to direct their attention to the screens where the cover and release date for her highly anticipated re-recording Speak Now (Taylor's Version) appeared.

July 7, 2023 - Kansas City, MO

The first Eras Tour show after the release of Speak Now (Taylor's Version). "Long Live" from the album was added to the Speak Now set.

August 9, 2023 - Los Angeles, CA

On the last night of the first US leg of the tour, before singing the first surprise song during the acoustic set, Taylor asked the crowd to once again direct their attention to the screen for a special announcement. The screen displayed the cover and release date for 1989 (Taylor's Version). There had been speculation of an announcement on this day as the date 8/9 resembled the 1989 title.

At the 2024 Grammy Awards Taylor announced her eleventh studio album, THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT.
